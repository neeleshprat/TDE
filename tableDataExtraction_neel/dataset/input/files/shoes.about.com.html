International Women's Shoe Sizes Conversion Chart

Compare Before Buying Online from Another Country

By Desiree Stimpert

If you plan to buy shoes online, or go on an international shoe shopping spree, you may want to consult the women's shoes size conversion chart below to get an idea of what your size might be in another country.

This shoe size chart converts women's shoe sizes from those of one country to those of another. I've used it several times myself with much success, and I've scored some great deals on shoes -- although, I must admit that I wear an average shoe size, and can usually buy off the rack without any trouble.

Better Safe Than Sorry
Of course, if you want to buy shoes online, and you want the perfect fit (especially if you wear large size women's shoes or wide women's shoes) you'll want to have your feet measured by a professional. Then, take those measurements and consult the manufacturer of the shoes you want to buy and, whichever online shoe store you order from, be sure you check their return policy.
