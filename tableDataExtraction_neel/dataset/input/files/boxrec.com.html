  • BoxRec News Young Fighter of the Year 2013: Callum Smith

    In 2013, Callum ‘Mundo’ Smith (9-0) gave a couple of hints to suggest that he is the future of the super middleweight division - not just domestically, but internationally too. BoxRec News writers were unanimous in their picks for the Young Fighter of the Year Award (23 or under on 31st December 2013).



    Starting his professional career just 14 months ago under Matchroom, in 2013 Smith managed seven straight knockout wins, taking a big step up when he faced Patrick Mendy for the English title in September. Former Prizefighter champion Mendy had never been stopped before, but was overwhelmed in the first round, taking a count before Smith continued with a relentless, come-forward demolition job which caused referee Victor Loughlin to wave it off just before the end on the opening round.
