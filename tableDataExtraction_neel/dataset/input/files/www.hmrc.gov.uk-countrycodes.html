EU country codes, VAT numbers and VAT in other languages

This table is to assist you in completing your EC Sales List (ESL). It includes European Union (EU) country codes, VAT number formats and links to foreign language enquiry letters.

Countries not listed in the table below are not part of the EU VAT area and so sales to those countries should not be included on your ESL. The following are not part of the EU VAT area:

  • Andorra
  • The Channel Islands
  • Gibraltar
  • Mount Athos
  • San Marino
  • Vatican City

The UK and the Isle of Man are part of the EU VAT area, but you should not include sales to the UK or the Isle of Man on your ESL.
